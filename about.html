Hello there!

My name is Anita; some people call me Annie.

I am currently studying Graphic Design at Algonquin College.

I've always enjoyed art, design and everything related. I can see beauty, design, art, and/or artistic qualities in almost anything.

It is my passion and lifestyle.

However, it took time to come to the realization that I wanted and needed a career in art and design.

Society has told us that a career in these fields is not one that will allow for a stable and successful living — minus some exceptions. With this in mind, I didn't know what I wanted to be when I grew up or what I wanted to do for the rest of my life, unlike everyone around me.

In highschool, I didn't know what I wanted to take in post-secondary school, so I took a variety of courses to keep my options open.

I took all of the Sciences, almost all of the Mathematics, and of course, I tried to take as many Arts courses as I could. Dance, Music, and Drama are considered Arts, but the ones I'm referring to are Visual Arts, Media Arts, and Computer Arts. I was also fortunate enough to take Communication Technology courses, which I've realized is very similar to Graphic Design, if not the same.

Design is often overlooked — in an under-appreciated sense. I overlooked design in not realizing its potential as the perfect career direction for myself. Now I know it is my passion.

Share what your passion is with me and I will use my passion and skills to inspire others to be just as passionate about your interests as you are.

Whether you need photographs taken, posters designed, websites created, or just want to make an object look beautiful instead of boring, I will meet — and try to exceed — your needs and standards.
